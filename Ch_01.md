### 在html中插入Js代码
````js
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
	<title>1-1-2</title>
</head>
<body>
	
</body>
<script type="text/javascript">
	alert("Hello Javascript!");
</script>
</html>
````
### 外部链接Js
````js
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
	<title>1-1-3</title>
</head>
<body>

</body>
<script type="text/javascript" src="js/1-3.js"></script>
</html>
````
